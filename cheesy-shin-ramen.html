Cheesy Shin Ramen

Ingredients

  • Shin Ramen Packet
  • 2 Slices of American Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 and 1/2 cup of water

Directions

  1. Add Shin Ramen noodles, the msg packet, and water in a bowl and put in microwave on high for 2 minutes and 30 seconds
  2. Add egg to bowl and microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds
  3. Break up cheese and add to bowl. Microwave 1 minute
