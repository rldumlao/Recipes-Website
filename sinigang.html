Sinigang

Ingredients

  • 2 Tamirand Packet
  • 2 Onions
  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 2 Tablespoon of diced ginger
  • 2 Tablespoons of diced garlic
  • 5 lb of pork ribs
  • 2 Potatoes
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 Lotus Root

Directions

  1. Cut Onions, Tomatoes, Potatoes, Carrots, and Lotus Root.
  2. Add all ingredient into a giant pot.
  3. Simmer for minimum of 2 hours.
  4. Serve with rice.
