Adobo

Ingredients

  • 5 lb of Chicken Thighs
  • 1/2 Cup of Vinegar
  • 1/2 Cup of Soy Sauce
  • 5 Crushed Garlic Cloves
  • 5 Bay Leaves
  • 1 Tablespoon of Black Pepper Balls

Directions

  1. Add 5 lb of chicken thighs into a large pot.
  2. Add 1/2 cup of vineger and 1/2 cup of so sauce.
  3. Add 5 crushed cloves of garlic, 5 bay leaves, and 1 tablespoon of black pepper balls.
  4. Heat pot at medium-high heat until boiling, then simmer for 2 hours.
  5. Cook until chicken is tender and then serve.
